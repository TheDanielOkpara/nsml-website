About This Property

Stormers Sports Club — formerly Nilayo Football Club — is NSML's flagship sports club property. The club has established itself as a powerhouse in Ogun State football, claiming back-to-back Lisabi Cup Championships in 2024 and 2025, and earning promotion to the Nigeria National League (NNL) of Nigeria's domestic football structure.

The Stormers Sports Club Academy is the club's golden asset. Built on a partnership with Baptist Boys High School in Abeokuta, the academy signs talented secondary school students under long-term contracts, developing the next generation of Nigerian football talent within a structured, professionally managed environment.

NSML's vision for Stormers extends beyond football. The club is integrating other sports engagements — including athletics, basketball, and volleyball — creating a multi-sport platform for talent development across Ogun State and Nigeria.
